  • R
    Role What job is this question trying to do? What role does the answer need to play?
  • F

    Intention Why is this question being asked now? What triggered this search moment?

The 7-Step Protocol

For each of the 98 states, we deploy a 7-step question resolution protocol. Here's how we surface and answer the deepest queries.

Step 01 — Input
"What keeps showing up no matter how hard you try to fix it? If I had a magic wand, what would disappear?"
Step 02 — Personify
"If this problem had a voice, what would it sound like? Is it a drill sergeant? A scared child?"
Step 03 — RFIP
"What is its Role, Function, Intention, and Purpose?"

"When did this first start? How old were you? What was happening then?"
Step 05 — Logic
"Given what was happening, does it make sense this pattern emerged?"
Step 06 — Update
"Look at the calendar. Look at the evidence. Are we still in that situation, or have things fundamentally changed?"

"Thank you for your service. The war is over. You can rest now."
