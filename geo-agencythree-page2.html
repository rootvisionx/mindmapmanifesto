// The Complete Framework

How We Achieve AI Visibility

A proven methodology combining macro-level psychological mapping with micro-level question resolution.

PILLAR 01

Answer Specific Questions

AI models are "answer engines." They look for content that has the exact framing of the question being asked. Stop creating vague titles—answer what your audience actually searches.

Instead of: "My Thoughts on Hypnosis"

Create: "How does hypnotherapy reprogram the subconscious?"
PILLAR 02

Transcripts Are Your Weapon

AI cannot "watch" video pixels—but it reads text instantly. Your transcript is the secret weapon. Publish full scripts on your website, not just auto-captions.

The Fix: Put full text in YouTube description + blog post embedded with video. Give AI text to read and cite.
PILLAR 03

Coin Your Own Terms

AI loves definitions. If you clearly define a term, the AI will use YOUR definition as the "fact." Create proprietary language and own the lexicon of your niche.

Format: "Faulty Subconscious Code is defined as [1-sentence]. It manifests as [list of 3 symptoms]."
PILLAR 04

Use Listicle Formatting

Clean lists are easier for AI to scrape and present than dense paragraphs. Structure beats prose. Break down every process into clear, numbered steps.

Structure: Step 1: Identify the Fear
Step 2: Map the Association
Step 3: Interrupt the Pattern
