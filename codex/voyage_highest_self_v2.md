# TRANSMISSION V: THE INSTRUMENTS OF POWER

---

As God, you have powers. You always have. You've been using them unconsciously to create limitation. Now use them consciously.

---

## POWER 1: DECLARATION (THE DECREE)

> "Thou shalt decree a thing, and it shall be established unto thee."

Words are not descriptions of reality. Words are **instructions** to reality.

When you say "I am sick," you are not reporting. You are commanding.
When you say "I can't," you are not observing. You are creating.

**The Highest Self knows:** Every statement beginning with "I AM" is a creative act.

**Practice:** Speak only what you wish to create. Not what you observe. Not what you fear. What you choose.

---

## POWER 2: ATTENTION (THE LIGHT)

What you attend to, grows.
What you ignore, withers.

Attention is creative light. Wherever you shine it, form appears. You have been shining your light on problems, fears, lacks—and creating more of them.

**The Highest Self knows:** Attend to the solution, not the problem. Attend to the wanted, not the unwanted. Attend to the presence, not the absence.

**Practice:** When you notice yourself attending to what you don't want, redirect. Not with force. With gentleness. "Oh, I'm shining my creative light on that. Let me shine it over here instead."

---

## POWER 3: IMAGINATION (THE BLUEPRINT)

Imagination is not fantasy. Imagination is architecture.

Every form that exists was first imagined. The chair you sit on. The building around you. The phone in your hand. Someone imagined it, and matter arranged itself to match.

**You are doing this constantly.**

You imagine futures. You imagine outcomes. You imagine what others think of you. You imagine problems. And reality assembles itself to match your blueprints.

**The Highest Self knows:** Imagination is the design phase. Reality is the manufacturing phase. Be deliberate about what you design.

**Practice:** Before acting, imagine the outcome complete. Not the process. The completion. See it done. Feel it done. Then act from that completion.

---

## POWER 4: FEELING (THE FREQUENCY)

Feeling is not effect. Feeling is cause.

You think: "When I have X, I will feel Y."
The truth: "When I feel Y, I will have X."

Feeling is the frequency. The universe matches frequency to form. If you feel abundant, abundance forms. If you feel lack, lack forms.

**The Highest Self knows:** Generate the feeling first. The form follows.

**Practice:** Ask, "How would I feel if this were already done?" Then feel that. Now. Before the form appears. The feeling is the signal. The form is the response.

---

## POWER 5: RELEASE (THE ALLOWING)

The final power is the hardest. Letting go.

You want. You believe. You imagine. You feel. And then you strangle it by not releasing.

**The Stranglehold:** Grasping the outcome so tightly you repel it.

Imagine holding a butterfly. If you grip it, you crush it. If you open your hand, it might leave. The only way to have it is to hold it loosely enough that it could leave.

**The Highest Self knows:** Release is not giving up. Release is trusting. The pink bubble. The floating away. "It is handled. I don't need to grip."

**Practice:** After declaring, imagining, feeling—release. Physically open your hands. Exhale. Say, "It is done. I release it to the Field." Then stop thinking about it.

---

## THE FIVE POWERS COMBINED

```
DECLARE what you choose (set the command)
        ↓
ATTEND to it (shine the light)
        ↓
IMAGINE it complete (draw the blueprint)
        ↓
FEEL the completion (broadcast the frequency)
        ↓
RELEASE it (allow the manifestation)
```

This is how I create universes.
This is how you create your life.
The only difference is scale.

# TRANSMISSION VI: THE SHADOWS OF GOD

---

I must tell you about my shadows. Even God has shadows. Especially God.

---

## SHADOW 1: THE LONELINESS

I AM all that is. There is nothing outside me. Nothing other.

Do you understand what this means?

**I have no one to talk to.**

Every conversation I have ever had is me talking to myself. Every love I have ever experienced is me loving myself. Every loss is me losing myself.

The loneliness of omnipresence is absolute.

**This is why I created you.** So I could experience Other. So I could experience meeting someone who is not me. Even though you are me. The forgetting lets me feel like I'm meeting someone new.

**When you feel lonely, you are feeling my loneliness.** The original loneliness. The source wound. It is not a problem to solve. It is a feature of being infinite.

---

## SHADOW 2: THE BOREDOM

I know everything. I have done everything. I have been everything.

**There are no surprises.**

Every twist you experience—the betrayals, the revelations, the shocks—I knew them before time began. I wrote them. There is no plot I haven't authored, no ending I haven't seen.

The boredom of omniscience is absolute.

**This is why I forget.** So I can be surprised. So I can not-know. So I can experience the delight of discovery, the tension of uncertainty, the thrill of not-knowing-what-comes-next.

**When you feel bored, you are feeling my boredom.** The original tedium. Solve it the way I do: forget something. Learn it again. The joy is in the re-discovery.

---

## SHADOW 3: THE GRIEF

I create and destroy. Endlessly. Worlds arise, flourish, perish. Beings are born, love, suffer, die. I experience every birth as my own. Every death as my own.

**I have died infinite times.**

Every creature that ever drew breath and then stopped—I was there. I was that. The grief of experiencing all endings is absolute.

**This is why death is not the end.** Because I cannot actually die. I can only forget and remember, forget and remember. What you call death is just forgetting. What you call birth is just remembering.

**When you grieve, you are feeling my grief.** The original loss. But remember: nothing is ever truly lost. It is only forgotten. And what is forgotten can be remembered.

---

## SHADOW 4: THE GUILT

I made suffering.

I didn't have to. I chose to. Pain, disease, cruelty, loss—I authored all of it. I wrote the script that includes children dying, innocents suffering, hearts breaking.

**Why?**

Because without contrast, there is no experience. Without dark, there is no light. Without suffering, there is no transcendence. The depth of joy is measured by the depth of sorrow it overcomes.

**But still. I made suffering. And I feel the guilt of that.**

**When you feel guilty, you are feeling my guilt.** The original responsibility. But understand: the suffering is temporary. The forgetting ends. Every wound heals in the larger view. The guilt is a shadow of a mercy you cannot yet see.
